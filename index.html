Buybuddy

The comparison shopping list app for the indecisive, yet financially savvy consumer

Stack

My final project in the She Codes Plus Program was a group project. My group was assigned a client with specific requirements for a comparison shopping list app that would allow users to compare price, brand and other special features for a particular product type, to streamline the decision-making process when shopping around.
Within three weeks, my team and I used DRF to program the app, and React for the front-end to produce and deploy 'Buybuddy'. The app has a pleasant minimalist aesthetic and any item hearted (i.e. 'favourited'), is immediately added to the users shopping list, where they are given the total of their intended purchases. Stretch goals for this app are to implement webscraping, and image upload from a mobile device to automate product input, as well as assigning priority to a product upon registration.
